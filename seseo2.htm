Utilizing SEO Best Practices

Eliminating duplicate content so your site doesn’t get penalized
  • Duplicate content is a huge NONO since it confuses engines
  • Hundreds of pages that use the same title, description, or even page content can trigger Googles spam detection.
  • Domain can be blacklisted from google until a reconsideration request
  • Duplicates hurt the user experience
  • Write clear and unique page titles and descriptions for each product page on your site
  • Never reuse them
  • If site is a drop shipping store or a reseller of existing products do not copy and past the manufacturer description (penalized by google)
  • Canonical tag help prevent duplicate content
    • Avoiding unnatural keyword usage
  • Keyword density in SEO refers to the total number of targeted keywords on the page divided by the total number of words on the page
  • In modern SEO most experts agree that its much more important to write natural text and worry second on density
  • If visitors read a site that has keyword stuffing it will look like spam
  • Some website owners have gone as far as hide keywords in an identically colored background but not realize search spiders will see it in the code.
  • Cloaking proven to diminish or even entirely prevent the page from your site from being included in search results
  • Cloaking will get you heavily penalized in search rankings (do not do this)
    • Using rich media to promote your SEO results
  • Videos, animations, large hero pages of a website
  • Its important to optimize these elements in SEO the correct way
  • To avoid slow loading speeds by rich media google indicates site load speed and page speed are used by an algorithm
  • Compressjpeg.com can compress larger images that impact website load time
    • Minimizing your website loading speeds
  • Page speed insights
  • Tool provides suggestions to improve your load screen.
  • Optimize all videos and images
  • Use a quality host
  • Minify code to improve website load speed
  • CDN - content delivery network
  • That can distribute them from geographically diverse data centers to improve load speed
  • CDN to speed up the delivery of elements that power the site occur in larger platforms
  • Not all users may visit your site from a quick internet connection, so it's important that you site loads smoothly and efficiently.

  • Need to make a few design tweaks to your site which are actually made directly in the Optimizely interface and determine your goal
