A. Kabeer Stories Presents:

Stuck in the Jar

All Cookies, come from a cookie jar.

We give the cookies their home.

In the real world cookies are considered one of the top desserts in the world. A variety of cookies are made across countries that are enjoyed by many. In our kitchens they are stored in cookie jars. Those cookie jars, are their homes.

Chocolate Chip is stuck in the jar

Of the many cookies made in the world Jerry the Chocolate Chip lives in his glass jar all by himself. His company are the crumbles but he is all alone. He watches the world pass by day by day waiting for the new cookies to join him in the jar. He has no idea what happens in the world around him, Jerry is stuck in his glass house unaware that milk cartons have taken over the world.

Charlie the Snickerdoodle hero

Charlie is a specially made hardcore ninja cookie who sees Jerry in his lonely jar. She believes that with the power of sugar she can save him from his glass home. The hardest journey in a cookies life starts from outside of their home and Charlie is determined to save all cookies despite their milk carton enemies.

Cookies were not harmed in the making
