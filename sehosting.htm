Web Hosting

After doing some research on my own I realize a lot of these web hosting sites really look like ads! They are filled will soft lines and different colors that I kinda feel like they are not legitimate. I found a free one: freewebhosting.com and it has a lot of features to help you get started on your website. This type of hosting looks closely similar to shared hosting, where it often comes with all the necessities like its own web builder, email, and access to sharing on social media. I researched another web hosting called SiteGround. This isnt free web hosting but it has three different tiers each rated with a different monthly plan. Usually the more you pay the more there is offered. The way it works for this one is that you select a plan, en a domain, and then you start building your site right away. During my research I found that the process for freewebhosting only allows the access to directly start building your website while you have to make an extra step of purchasing your domain. So its not really free entirely since the domain needs to be purchased. Siteground has their plans differently. They have a variety of different web hosting. The cheapest is shared hosting ranging from $10-$30 a month to dedicated servers that range from $230 to $430. There is one more level that allows a custom service and im pretty sure that number goes higher.
